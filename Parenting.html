Teen Parenting?

1

The Child's Life

Even for decisions they may not be able to make for themselves, parents have the right to make decisions for their children

(Ex: Healthcare)

2

Parental Rights

Both parents, the father and the mother have the right to be a part of the child's life.


With the difficulty of teen parenting both parents are guaranteed to be able to take care of the child

3

Adoption & Specifics

Teen parents have the right and the option to send their kid out to adoption


Children born to certain minors, are taken custody of and put into the adoption system immediately


This is to preemptively avoid unsafe environments from being exposed to child
